# 📋 Reporting

Zephyr takes rule violations seriously — but reporting must be done responsibly and in good faith. Abuse of the reporting system is itself a punishable offence.

***

**⏱️ Report Window**

* All rule breaches must be reported within **24 hours** of the incident occurring
* Reports submitted outside of this window will **not be actioned**
* Time-stamps, video evidence, and context are expected wherever possible

***

**🚫 Revenge Reporting**

* Submitting a report in retaliation for being reported yourself is **strictly prohibited**
* Reports that are found to be retaliatory in nature will be dismissed and may result in a **strike against the reporter**

***

**📌 What to Report**

* Serious rule breaches such as:
  * Metagaming
  * RDM / VDM
  * Fail RP
  * Power gaming
  * Cheating or exploitation
* Minor disputes or in-character grievances should be resolved **through RP** — not tickets

***

**📝 How to Report**

* Open a ticket in the appropriate Discord channel
* Provide the following:
  * Your in-game name
  * The offending player's name
  * Date and time of the incident
  * A clear description of what occurred
  * Supporting evidence (video clips preferred)

***

**⚠️ False or Malicious Reports**

* Deliberately filing false reports to target or harass another player will result in disciplinary action
* Staff reserve the right to investigate the reporter if a report appears fabricated or in bad faith
