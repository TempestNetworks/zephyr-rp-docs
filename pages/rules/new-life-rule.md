# 🔁 New Life Rule (NLR)

The New Life Rule (NLR) applies whenever your character fully respawns or is declared deceased. If this occurs, your character is considered to have no memory of the events leading up to their death or incapacitation. You may not return to the scene, continue the situation, seek revenge, recover lost items, or use information gained prior to your death.

If your character is revived by EMS, you retain memory of the situation and events that occurred prior to being downed. However, being revived does not allow you to immediately return to or re-engage in the active scenario. Once treated, you are expected to remove yourself from the situation and allow the roleplay to naturally conclude without further involvement from your character.

Repeatedly returning to ongoing shootouts, conflicts, police scenes, or hostile situations after being downed or revived will be considered a breach of NLR and may result in staff action.

## ⚠️ Severe Injury / DOA Clause

If your injuries are considered too severe to realistically justify revival, EMS may declare your character DOA (Dead on Arrival). In these situations, your character will be treated as fully deceased under NLR and you will lose all memory of the roleplay scenario and events leading up to it.
