# 🫡 Respect for Staff & Community

Zephyr Roleplay is built around creating a respectful, enjoyable, and immersive environment for everyone involved. All members of the community — including players, staff, and content creators — are expected to treat each other with maturity and respect at all times.

The following behaviour is not tolerated:
- Harassment or bullying
- Discriminatory or derogatory language
- Personal attacks or insults
- Trolling, griefing, or intentionally disruptive behaviour
- Cheating or exploiting
- Attempting to create, encourage, or spread community drama

Instigating unnecessary drama, conflict, or hostility within the community may result in punishment or removal from Zephyr Roleplay.

If you have issues with another player or a staff member, they should be handled calmly and through the appropriate support channels. Public arguments, callouts, or attempts to rally community members against others are not acceptable and only damage the community environment.

---

# 🎭 Roleplay Standards

Zephyr aims to provide players with a genuine, immersive, and realistic roleplaying experience.

Players are expected to:
- Prioritise roleplay over “winning”
- Prefer words over weapons wherever possible
- Create meaningful and believable interactions
- Contribute positively to scenes and story progression

While criminal roleplay is a major part of the server, players should always aim to create engaging and high-quality RP rather than rushing directly to violence or low-effort interactions.

---

# 🔞 Age Requirement

All players must be at least **16 years of age** to access and play Zephyr Roleplay.
