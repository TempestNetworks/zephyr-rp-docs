# 🕵️‍♂️ Cheating

Cheating in any form is strictly prohibited within Zephyr Roleplay. This includes, but is not limited to, the use of hacks, scripts, mod menus, third-party software, exploits, or any external assistance used to gain an unfair advantage over other players.

We expect all players to engage in fair and honest gameplay at all times. Cheating damages the integrity of the server, negatively impacts the experience of others, and will result in severe punishment, including permanent bans.

Exploiting bugs or unintended game mechanics for personal gain is also considered cheating and may result in punishment.

Due to the increasing difficulty in detecting cheats and unfair software, Zephyr Roleplay may require players who are involved in suspicious kill reports to provide POV clips upon request. These clips must clearly show the relevant engagement and be a minimum of **30 seconds in length**. Players are strongly encouraged to clip their gameplay if they believe their actions may be questioned.

Failure to provide requested POV footage may result in punishment, including bans, where staff believe sufficient suspicion exists. This system was implemented following community feedback and voting to help maintain fair and competitive gameplay standards.

> The Zephyr Management Team reserves the right to remove any player considered high-risk, suspicious, or otherwise deemed a threat to the fairness, integrity, or competitive environment of the server.
