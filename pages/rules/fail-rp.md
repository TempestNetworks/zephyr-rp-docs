# 🎭 Fail RP  
Fail RP refers to actions that break immersion, realism, or logical roleplay expectations.  
Players are expected to value their character’s life, injuries, and the world around them at all times.

Fail RP includes, but is not limited to:

- Ignoring or downplaying serious injuries  
- Acting without fear or self-preservation in life-threatening situations  
- Unrealistic reactions to being threatened, restrained, or injured  
- Instantly recovering from situations that would reasonably impact your character  
- Addressing players by their Steam/Discord unless it's their nickname  
- Ramming or pitting at speeds exceeding 150 KM/h  
- Carrying whilst in the driver seat of ANY vehicle  
- Using unrealistic full face paint or excessive facial makeup that promotes “fragger” style gameplay

Examples of Fail RP:

- Being shot and immediately sprinting or fighting without medical attention  
- Treating serious injuries as minor inconveniences  
- Laughing, joking, or taunting in situations where fear or distress would be expected  
- Returning to normal activity immediately after near-death situations

# 🎬 Low Quality RP

Low Quality RP refers to unrealistic, poorly developed, or unnecessarily escalated roleplay scenarios that negatively impact the experience of others.

Players are expected to create meaningful and believable roleplay rather than prioritising constant shootouts or “win mentality” gameplay.

Low Quality RP includes, but is not limited to:

- Shooting Police over minor offences such as traffic stops or routine interactions  
- Escalating situations to deadly force without meaningful roleplay progression  
- Creating hostilities with little to no prior interaction or build-up  
- Prioritising gunplay over character development and roleplay  
- Engaging in repetitive “fragger” style behaviour with minimal roleplay intent
- Interjecting in other players RP scenes unnecessarily 
   Example: Seeing someone in a police chase and ramming PD or the player being chased to impact the scene.

Examples of Low Quality RP:

- Opening fire on Police because you are being fined or pulled over  
- Immediately resorting to violence during basic disagreements  
- Creating conflict purely for loot, gunfights, or kill clips  
- Constantly seeking hostile interactions without meaningful reasoning

Failure to follow this rule may result in strikes for your group and individual bans.
